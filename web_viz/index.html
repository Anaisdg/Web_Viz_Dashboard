Summary: Lattitude vs. X

The purpose of this project was to analye the weather along the equator. Weather data was retrieved from the OpenWeatherMap API. 500+ latitudes and longitudes were randomly generated and CityPy was used to find the nearest city. Matplotlib and Pandas was used to generate a series of scatter plots that display the relationship between weather and latitude. This web vizualization dashboard highlights the relationship between latitude and temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed.
